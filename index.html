STUDY HABITS

It is a practice and routine behaviors that individuals use to learn, apply new information, and skills or knowledge. It can also enhance learning outcomes, improve academic performance, and help individuals to stay organized and manage their time efficiently. By learning good study habits, individuals especially the students can optimize their learning process and achieve their academic goals.

LIST OF EFFECTIVE STUDY HABITS

Time management study habits

  • Prioritization - prioritize tasks based on its importance and deadlines.
  • Create a study schedule - Plan out dedicated study time for each subject.
  • Set time limits - Allocate specific time for each task to stay focused.

Active Learning Techniques

  • Summarization - Write what you've learned in a summary to improve understanding.
  • Active Recall - Recall information from memory rather than re-reading it.
  • Discuss with others - To explore topics and gain new insights.

Productivity and Focus Habits

  • Short breaks - Take a short break to rest and recharge to avoid burn out.
  • Minimize distraction - Create a quiet study space and limit digital distraction to focus.
  • Mindfulness and Focus - incorporate mindful techniques to improve your concentration.
